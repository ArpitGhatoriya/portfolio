About Me

I'm Arpit Pannalal Ghatoriya — a Product Marketing and Growth professional based in the San Francisco Bay Area with 4+ years of experience turning data into strategy that actually moves the needle.

My career has spanned global enterprise brands like Disney+, TikTok, and P&G at Publicis Groupe, to building digital presence from scratch for CPG brands like Aashirvaad, to defining product strategy at an early-stage AI startup. What connects it all: I believe the best marketing is built on a foundation of clean data, sharp positioning, and relentless experimentation.
